KeenFresh User Guide

Master your remote desktop with advanced gestures and shortcuts.

👆 Trackpad Gestures (Default Mode)

Single Tap

Performs a standard Left Click anywhere on the screen.

Two-Finger Tap

Performs a standard Right Click to open context menus.

Three-Finger Tap

Instantly Toggles the UI Overlay on and off.

Long Press & Drag

Hold for 0.5s until vibration, then move to Drag and Drop windows or files.

Two-Finger Pan

Smoothly Scroll up, down, left, or right.

🔍 Advanced View Controls

Pinch to Zoom

Use two fingers to dynamically zoom into the screen up to 2.5x scale.

Direct Touch Mode

Absolute pointer mapping where taps instantly teleport the cursor.

Clipboard Sync
