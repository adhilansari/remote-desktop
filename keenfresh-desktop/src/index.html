KeenFresh User Manual

Master your remote desktop with our advanced gestures and hidden features.

👆 Core Trackpad Gestures (Default Mode)

Single Tap

Performs a standard Left Click exactly where the cursor is currently located.

Two-Finger Tap

Performs a Right Click to quickly open context menus.

Three-Finger Tap

Instantly Toggles the Top Menu and UI overlay on and off.

Long Press & Drag

Hold for 0.5s until you feel a vibration, then move your finger to Drag and Drop windows or select text.

Two-Finger Pan

Slide two fingers together to smoothly Scroll up, down, left, or right.

Three-Finger Swipe

Swipe horizontally with three fingers to instantly Switch Displays/Monitors.

🔍 Advanced Display & View Controls

Pinch to Zoom

Use two fingers to dynamically zoom into the screen up to 2.5x scale. Useful for reading small text.

Direct Touch Mode

Switches from relative trackpad mode to absolute mapping. Tapping the screen instantly teleports the cursor to that location.

Stream Quality Settings

Open the "More Options" menu to adjust quality between 1080p, 720p, or Data Saver mode if your network is slow.

⌨️ Productivity Tools & Shortcuts

Quick Macros & Modifiers

Open the Keyboard panel from the top bar to access instant shortcuts (Ctrl, Alt, Shift, Copy, Paste, Undo).

Clipboard Sync

Copy text on your PC, and it instantly syncs to your phone. Use the "Paste from phone" option in the menu to send text back.

System Actions

You can Lock, Sleep, and Unlock the remote PC securely directly from the power menu in the mobile app.
